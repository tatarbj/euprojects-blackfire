11.1Field labeled align #permalink

Labeled align modifier should be used for aligning fields that do not have a label with fields that do have a label.

Example
Phone number
+48531555003
Some text that gets aligned to the right 
<div class="field field--labeled">
  <div class="field__label">Phone number</div>
  <div class="field__items">
    +48531555003
  </div>
</div>

<div class="field field--labeled-align">
  <div class="field__items">
    Some text that gets aligned to the right
  </div>
</div>
